Atomic Swap Phases

MAKER SIDE (you post an offer)

1. TAKE RECEIVED
A taker accepted your offer via Nostr.

2. MY HTLC LOCKED
You lock your funds on-chain in an HTLC.

3. WAITING TAKER LOCK
Waiting for taker to lock their side.

4. WAITING TAKER CLAIM
Taker claims your HTLC, revealing the preimage.

5. CLAIM OTHER CHAIN
You use the revealed preimage to claim taker's funds.

6. COMPLETE ✓
TAKER SIDE (you accept an offer)

1. TAKE SENT
You accept an offer, notify the maker via Nostr.

2. WAITING MAKER LOCK
Waiting for maker to lock funds on-chain.

3. CONFIRMING HTLC
Waiting 1 confirmation on maker's HTLC (anti double-spend).

4. LOCK MY HTLC
You lock your funds on the other chain.

5. CLAIM
You claim maker's HTLC using the preimage.

6. COMPLETE ✓

If either party disappears, funds auto-refund after the timelock expires. No funds are ever at risk.

How Atomic Swaps Work

Offers are peer-to-peer — no middleman, no custody.
Your offer stays live while this page is open (re-broadcasts every 5 min).
When you close the page, your offer disappears.

BCH ↔ BTC: Hash time-locked contracts (HTLC) + SHA-256.
BCH ↔ XMR: Cross-curve adaptor sigs (Schnorr + Ed25519 DLEQ).

Both sides lock funds on-chain. If either disappears, funds auto-refund after timelock.

XMR Note: The XMR wallet scans the last ~33 hours of blocks. If your XMR was received earlier, send it to yourself first so it appears as a fresh output.
