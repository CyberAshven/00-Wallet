How Auto Stealth Works
When you receive BCH, Auto Stealth runs a multi-round CoinJoin pipeline: your UTXOs are pooled with other participants and outputs are shuffled via onion encryption — nobody (not even participants) can link inputs to outputs. Each round's output lands on a one-time stealth address derived via ECDH, so only you can detect and spend it. More rounds = deeper privacy.

How Monero Works
✕
Block Scanning
Monero is private — your balance isn't on a public ledger. The wallet scans the blockchain from your wallet's creation height to find your outputs (~5 MB download).
Pre-existing Funds
If you imported an existing Monero address, funds received before this wallet was created here won't appear. Only transactions after the restore height are detected.
Unlock Period
After sending XMR, your change output is locked for 10 confirmations (~20 min). During this time, you can't send again.
